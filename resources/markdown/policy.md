# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Defrag Racing ("defrag.racing", "the Service", "We", "Us") collects, uses, and protects Your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

When You register, We collect:

* **Email address** - for account authentication and notifications
* **Username** - publicly visible on the site
* **Password** - stored as a secure hash, never in plain text

### 1.2 OAuth / Social Login Data

If You connect via Discord, Twitch, Steam, or Twitter/X, We receive a unique identifier and basic profile information (username, avatar) from those services. We do not receive or store Your passwords from these providers.

### 1.3 Game Data

We collect and display Quake 3 Defrag gameplay data including:

* Speedrun records and times (sourced from q3df.org and uploaded demos)
* Player aliases and nicknames (from game servers and records)
* Server activity and player presence on game servers
* Demo files uploaded by users

This data is inherently public within the Defrag community and is collected to provide rankings, profiles, and record tracking.

### 1.4 Uploaded Content

Content You upload - including demo files, player models, maplists, profile images, and backgrounds - is stored on Our servers and may be publicly accessible.

### 1.5 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

* IP address (used for rate limiting and abuse prevention)
* Pages visited and actions taken
* Browser type and device information

### 1.6 Donations

If You make a donation, We store the donation amount, currency, and associate it with Your account. Payment processing is handled by third-party providers - We do not store payment card details.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

* Provide and maintain the Service (profiles, rankings, records, demos)
* Process and display speedrun records and rankings
* Render demo files into YouTube videos
* Enable community features (maplists, headhunter, marketplace, clans, tournaments)
* Prevent abuse and enforce rate limits (download/upload limits)
* Send notifications about Your records, demos, and account activity
* Improve the Service through usage analysis

## 3. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:

* **Session management** - keeping You logged in
* **Preferences** - remembering Your settings (physics order, layout, filters)
* **CSRF protection** - preventing cross-site request forgery

We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell Your personal data. We may share data in the following cases:

* **Public profiles** - Your username, game records, rankings, and uploaded content are publicly visible by design
* **YouTube** - Rendered demo videos are published to YouTube, displaying map names, player names, and times
* **Game servers** - Server listings display player names currently connected to game servers (this data is publicly queryable from the game servers themselves)
* **Legal requirements** - We may disclose data if required by law

## 5. Data Retention

* **Account data** is retained for as long as Your account is active
* **Game records** are retained indefinitely as part of the community's historical archive
* **Uploaded demos** are retained indefinitely unless deleted by the uploader or removed for violations
* **Usage logs** (IP addresses, access logs) are retained for up to 90 days
* **Deleted accounts** - upon account deletion, personal data is removed but game records (which are public community data) may be retained in anonymized form

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure password hashing, and access controls. However, no system is 100% secure, and We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

* **Access** Your personal data through Your profile settings
* **Update** Your account information at any time
* **Delete** Your account by contacting Us
* **Export** Your data by contacting Us
* **Object** to processing by contacting Us

For GDPR-related requests, contact us at [admin@defrag.racing](mailto:admin@defrag.racing).

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If We discover such data has been collected, We will delete it promptly.

## 9. Third-Party Links

The Service contains links to external sites (Discord, YouTube, Twitch, q3df.org, game server connections). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance.
